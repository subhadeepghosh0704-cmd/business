About Us

Our Story

Founded by Subhadeep Ghosh, Buildwave Tech emerged with a simple goal: to provide businesses with enterprise-grade digital infrastructure. We bridge the gap between stunning UI/UX design and powerful, complex backend systems.

Based in Madhyamgram, Kolkata, we have quickly grown into a trusted technology partner for educational institutes, corporate businesses, and emerging startups.

Mission

To empower businesses globally through scalable, secure, and innovative software solutions and high-converting digital marketing strategies.

Vision

To be recognized as the leading tech agency known for architectural excellence and transformative digital results.
